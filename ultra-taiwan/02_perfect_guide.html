üöÜGetting to the Party
Your Journey to Ultra Taiwan: A Transportation Guide

Getting to Ultra Taiwan 2024 is an adventure in itself, and I'm here to guide you through all your transportation options to ensure you arrive ready to party!

Taking the Metro - The Local's Choice
The easiest and most reliable way to reach the festival is via Taipei's excellent metro system. Simply hop on the Red Line and make your way to Yuanshan Station. Once there, head out through Exit 1, and you'll likely hear the distant thump of bass and see fellow ravers heading to the venue. It's just a scenic 15-minute walk from there. The metro runs from 6 AM until midnight, perfect for both early birds and night owls. Here's a pro tip: grab an EasyCard to make your journey smoother and avoid queuing for single tickets.

Ultra Festival Shuttle Service
For those wanting a more direct route, the festival's official shuttle service is your best friend. These dedicated party buses run continuously from Taipei Main Station between 2 PM and 11 PM, with departures every 20 minutes. At just NT$50 per trip, it's incredibly cost-effective. Just remember to secure your shuttle passes early, as they tend to sell out quickly during peak times.

Ride-Sharing and Taxis
If comfort is your priority, ride-sharing services and taxis are readily available. Expect to pay between NT$250-300 for your journey.
